## Project Overview

The project involved extensive use of CAD modeling, and 3D printing to prototype and finalize the design for a Howimat-inspired gear reducer. The Howimat design uses ball bearings driven by a cam to achieve zero-low backlash in a minimal profile. It is inspired by the gear reducer Dylan Edmiston used in this [video](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=bZBH35Yfn6k) and what is used in the highest end Heller CNC machines as seen [here](https://www.detlevhofmann.de/products/howimat-gear-type-units/?lang=en).

## Key Achievements

* CAD modelling of advanced shapes, such as the spline and cam
* Section analysis of final model to verify ball bearing movement
* 3D printing prototypes and final part
